# 🍽️ One-Pan Garlic Butter Salmon

Perfect for weeknights or when you want to impress someone in under 30 minutes (including yourself).

## 🛒 Ingredients

- 2 salmon fillets  
- 3 garlic cloves, minced  
- 2 tbsp butter  
- 1 lemon, sliced  
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped  
- Salt & pepper to taste  

<NutritionInfo
  calories={480}
  protein="34g"
  carbs="2g"
  fat="38g"
/>

## 🔥 Instructions

1. **Prep the salmon**: Pat dry and season both sides with salt and pepper.
2. **Sear**: In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
3. **Cook**: Add salmon fillets skin-side down. Sear for 4-5 minutes.
4. **Flip & finish**: Add lemon slices, baste with butter, cook for 3–4 more minutes.
5. **Serve** with chopped parsley on top.
